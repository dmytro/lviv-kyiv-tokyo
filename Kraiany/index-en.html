This is homepage of Ukrainian community in Japan

Community "Kraiany":

Statement of mission:
  • Helping and developing communications among Ukrainians in Japan;
  • Educating Ukrainians about Japanese culture;
  • Educating Japanese about Ukrainian culture;
  • Helping Ukrainians to adapt to Japanese way of life;
  • Strengthening cultural links between people of two nations.

Mailing list "Kraiany"

Mailing list "Kraiany" devoted to community life of Ukrainians in Japan. Mailing list suggested audience:

  • Ukrainians residing in Japan, both temporarily and permanently;
  • Ukrainians that are interested in Japanese culture and Japanese way of life;
  • Japanese people interested in Ukrainian culture, Ukrainian traditions and way of life;
  • people of other nationalities interested in strengthening and expanding bilateral cultural links between Ukraine and Japan.

To subscribe to the mailing list send message to address

  Majordomo@ml.asahi-net.or.jp
In the message body (not Subject line) put one line: 
  subscribe kraiany
or 
  subscribe kraiany  [MAIL-ADDRESS]
where [MAIL-ADDRESS] is your e-mail address. In some cases it is preferable to use second form, mostly when you're sending your subscription mail message from one address but would like to receive messages on another one.

To send message to the milling list

send your messages to following address: 
   kraiany@ml.asahi-net.or.jp

To cancel mailing list subscription

send a message 
   unsubscribe kraiany
to address 
   Majordomo@ml.asahi-net.or.jp
Please, note that addresses to (un)subscribe and mailing list address are different. Please, do not send command mails (subscribe/unsubscribe mails) to maillist address.
