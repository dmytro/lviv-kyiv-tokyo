June, 30 1999
<p>
<b>ANNOUNCE:</b> Linux-Mandrake development version now available in real time.
<p>
We, at MandrakeSoft, are very pleased to announce that we make available our developent version of Linux-Mandrake for the Linux community, in real time.
<p>
The development version is called "cooker" and is already available from several FTP mirrors. It's updated every hour with the last modifications.
<p>
The purpose of Cooker is to improve the Linux-Mandrake distribution by permitting a better interaction between our team and the Mandrake users, both for debugging and adding new features.
<p>
 HOW TO TEST IT?
<p>
Subscribe to the "cooker" list for bug-reports. Then, subscribe to "changelog" so you can be informed of any modification made to the cooker.
